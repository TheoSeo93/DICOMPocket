# DICOMPocket

DICOM Pocket processes selected DICOM, ZIP, and image files locally on the user’s device.

The app does not require an account, does not upload medical files to a server, and does not sell user data.

Exported PNG or ZIP files are created locally. Sharing or emailing only happens after the user chooses to share the exported file.
## Data Retention and Deletion

DICOM Pocket processes DICOM files locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or retain your DICOM files, medical images, studies, series, or related metadata on any servers.

## Data Retention and Deletion

DICOM Pocket is a local, read-only DICOM viewer. The app opens DICOM files that you select from your device, file manager, or another app.

DICOM Pocket does not upload, collect, copy, store, or retain your DICOM files, medical images, studies, series, or related metadata on our servers.

DICOM files are read only for the active viewing session. The app does not keep a permanent copy of the selected files after viewing. The original files remain in their original location, such as your device storage, file manager, cloud drive app, or the app you opened them from.

Because DICOM Pocket does not retain user files or account data on our servers, there is no server-side user data for us to delete. To delete a DICOM file, delete the original file from the location where it is stored. 



The app is not intended for medical diagnosis.
